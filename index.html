Who am I?

My name is Mark Bertoncelj and I am a Slovenian DJ / producer. You may know me under my stage name Berti. My DJ journey began in 2015. What started off as a passion became my life drive. Nothing brings a smile to my face more than seeing a crowd groove to the rhytms I put out. In late 2020 I co-founded a techno event organisation group called Lawaai. Now, I am considered one of the resident DJs at the events we organise.

What kind of music do I play?

I am mostly known for playing hard, robust techno. I also like to think that I excel at mixing house and tech-house tracks. You can find a lot of my DJ sets on the youtube channel I manage.

What kind of music do I like?

Most people do not now that I actually have a really wide spectre of music I enjoy - it depends on the occasion. Some of the genres I like to listen to include rock, pop, grunge, deep house, tech-house, EDM, lo-fi etc.
